# Product Requirements Document: Babinraj V Portfolio Website

## 1. Project Overview
The goal is to develop a high-end, interactive personal portfolio for Babinraj V, a Senior Associate Technology L1 at Publicis Sapient with over 7 years of experience. The website will serve as a digital narrative of his transition from a System Engineer at TCS to a Senior Java Developer and Test Automation expert.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Technical Recruiters**: Looking for specific stacks like Java 8, Spring Boot, and Microservices.
- **Engineering Managers**: Interested in ROI-driven results and AI-led process improvements.
- **Open Source Community**: For potential collaboration on GitHub.

- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 compliance for screen readers.
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first design, ensuring the complex animations degrade gracefully on low-powered devices.
- **Contact**: Direct integration with email (babinrajv@gmail.com) and phone (+91 8086298669).

- **Frontend**: Next.js (for SEO and Speed).
